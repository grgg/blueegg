Bio

Grant Goddard is a producer and musician from Los Angeles California. His focus in synthesis is centered around modular systems. He also works and produces in Ableton Live.

He's working on producing electronic music that can be used as pieces in hybrid modular / DJ performance, think techno tracks on CDJs synced to modular synth and drum voices.

He's finally taking music seriously.

Grant Goddard — Munich, 2024
