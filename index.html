Bio

Grant Goddard is a producer and musician from Los Angeles California. His focus in synthesis is centered around modular systems. He also works and produces in Ableton Live.

He's working on producing electronic music that can be used as pieces in hybrid modular / DJ performance. He's finally taking music seriously.
